About SABBTCo

SABBTCo is a trusted sourcing and freight forwarding company helping businesses and individuals import goods from China to Kenya with confidence, speed, and transparency.

Company Overview

SABBTCo specializes in product sourcing, supplier verification, freight forwarding, customs clearance, shipment tracking, and door-to-door delivery. We bridge the gap between Chinese manufacturers and East African buyers by providing secure and efficient logistics solutions.

Our Mission

To simplify global trade by making sourcing, shipping, and delivery from China to Kenya affordable, transparent, and reliable for every client.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted cross-border sourcing and logistics partner, connecting businesses to global supply chains efficiently.

Our Location

We operate between Shenzhen, China and Nairobi, Kenya, enabling seamless sourcing, freight consolidation, and delivery.

Our Services

  • Product sourcing from China
  • Supplier verification
  • Air freight
  • Sea freight
  • Customs clearance
  • Shipment tracking
  • Door-to-door delivery
